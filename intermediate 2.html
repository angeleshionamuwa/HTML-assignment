Let's add a receipe for some delicious Milkshake
We are going to borrow a nice picture from google

Here's what the recipe calls for

Ingredients

  • 4 large scoops(about 1 1/2c) vanilla icecream
  • 1/4 cups milk
  • Whipped topping, for garnishing
  • Sprinkles, for garish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

instructions

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
